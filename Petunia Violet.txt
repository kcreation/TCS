                Species:Violacea         Family:Solanaceae
                        Binomial name:Petunia violacea
Description:
    Most types of cultivated Petunia are hybrids of Petunia violacea, which 
has a purple flower,and Petunia axillaris,which has a white flower.
P.violacea is a perrenial herb in the wild(cultivated Petunias are annuals) 
that branches from the base and grows up to 60cm tall.The leaves are ovate 
and alternate.The plant is very heat tolerant,low spreading,and  blooms 
with a profusion of purple flowers.
