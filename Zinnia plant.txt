Description:
       Zinnia flowers are actually an herb that belongs to the aster family. 
They are named after the German botanist Johann Gottfried Zinn. 
There are both annual and perennial zinnia plants.These originally came 
from Mexico and are notable for their single, long-stemmed flowers 
that are available in a variety of different colors.
