Description:
           Ageratum(whiteweed in the USA), is a genus of 40 to 60 tropical 
and warm temperate flowering annuals and perennials from the family 
Asteraceae, tribe Eupatorieae. Most species are native to Central America
 and Mexico but four are native to the United States.They form tussocks
 or small hills.
