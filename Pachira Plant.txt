Description:
       Pachira aquatica is a tropical wetland tree of the mallow family 
Malvaceae, native to Central and South America where it grows in 
swamps. It is known by the common names Malabar chestnut, 
French peanut, Guiana chestnut, provision tree, saba nut, monguba (Brazil), 
pumpo (Guatemala) and is commercially sold under the names money plants.
