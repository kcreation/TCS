                             Binomial name: Aster amellus L.
Description:
          Aster is a genus of flowering plants in the family Asteraceae. 
Its circumscription has been narrowed, and it now encompasses 
around 180 species, all but one of which are restricted to Eurasia;
 many species formerly in Aster are now in other genera of the 
tribe Astereae.
