Description:
    Aegle marmelos, commonly known as bael (or bili or bhel), also Bengal 
   quince,golden apple,Japanese bitter orange,stone apple or 
   wood apple,is a species of tree native to India, 
   Nepal, the Andaman and Nicobar Islands and Myanmar.
   It is present in Sri Lanka,Thailand and Malesia as a naturalized species.
   The tree is considered to be sacred by Hindus.
