Description:
            Bauhinia variegata is a species of flowering plant in the family 
Fabaceae.It is native to South Asia and Southeast Asia, from southern 
China, Burma, India, Nepal, Pakistan, and Sri Lanka. Common names
 include orchid tree, camel's foot tree, kachnar and mountain-ebony.
