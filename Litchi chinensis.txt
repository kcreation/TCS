Description:
         Lychee (variously spelled litchi, liechee, liche, lizhi or li zhi, 
or lichee)is the sole member of the genus Litchi in the soapberry 
family, Sapindaceae.It is a tropical tree native to the Guangdong 
and Fujian provinces of China,where cultivation is documented 
from 1059 AD.
