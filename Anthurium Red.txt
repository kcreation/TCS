Description:
      Anthurium is a genus of about 1000 species of flowering plants, the 
largest genus of the arum family, Araceae. General common names include 
anthurium, tailflower, flamingo flower, and laceleaf. The genus is native 
to the Americas, where it is distributed from northern Mexico to 
northern Argentina and parts of the Caribbean.
