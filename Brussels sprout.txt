                                            Brussels sprout

                               Scientific classification
Species:Brassica oleracea   Cultivar group:Gemmifera Group
Origin:Low Countries
                Binomial name:  Brassica oleracea var. gemmifera
Description:
          Brussels sprouts (Brassica oleracea, variety gemmifera), 
form of cabbage, belonging to the mustard family Brassicaceae,
 widely grown in Europe and North America for its edible buds 
called “sprouts.” Brussels sprouts may have been grown in Belgium 
as early as 1200, but the first recorded description of it dates to 1587.
